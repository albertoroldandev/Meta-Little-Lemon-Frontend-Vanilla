Welcome to Little Lemon: A Taste of Mexico!

Our Menu

Explore our diverse menu featuring a fusion of classic and innovative Mexican dishes. From sizzling fajitas to tangy ceviches, handmade tacos to hearty enchiladas, every bite at Little Lemon tells a story of culture, flavor, and dedication to quality. Vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options are also available, ensuring there's something for every palate.

Salads

Savor freshness in every bite with our Salad! Crisp mixed greens, ripe cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, and bell peppers mingle in our tangy citrus vinaigrette. Topped with avocado, roasted pepitas, and crumbled queso fresco, this salad is a burst of flavors and textures, perfect as a refreshing starter or a satisfying main course.

Nachos

Dive into a fiesta of flavors with our Nachos! Crispy tortilla chips piled high with savory seasoned beef or tender grilled chicken, smothered in melted cheese, fresh salsa, jalapeños, and creamy guacamole. Each bite is a perfect blend of crunch, spice, and cheesiness—a crowd-pleasing appetizer that captures the essence of Mexican indulgence in every crunchy chip. Perfect for sharing or as a hearty individual delight!

Quesadillas

Treat yourself to the savory goodness of our Quesadillas! Melted cheese embraced between perfectly grilled flour tortillas, generously stuffed with your choice of succulent marinated steak, tender chicken, or a medley of sautéed vegetables. Served with sides of zesty salsa, sour cream, and fresh guacamole, these quesadillas are a delightful blend of gooey, flavorful cheese and your favorite fillings, creating a satisfying taste sensation with every bite.

Tacos

Satisfy your cravings with our authentic Tacos! Handcrafted soft corn tortillas cradle a symphony of flavors, from our tender, slow-cooked meats—choose from juicy carnitas, flavorful barbacoa, or spicy grilled shrimp—to the vibrant toppings of fresh cilantro, diced onions, and zesty lime wedges. Each taco is a celebration of Mexican street food, delivering a burst of savory, tangy, and aromatic goodness in every bite. Dive into a taco experience that transports your taste buds to the heart of Mexico!

Cheesecakes

Indulge in creamy decadence with our Cheesecake! Each velvety slice is a symphony of rich, smooth cream cheese atop a buttery graham cracker crust, perfectly balanced with a hint of vanilla. Whether you savor it plain or opt for our luscious fruit compote topping—strawberry, blueberry, or raspberry—the harmony of flavors will tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving one more forkful of this heavenly delight. Treat yourself to a slice of pure dessert bliss!

About Little Lemon

Little Lemon was born out of a dream to share the rich tapestry of Mexican cuisine with the world. Founded in 2020, our journey began with a commitment to sourcing the freshest ingredients and crafting traditional recipes passed down through generations. With each dish, we aim to transport our guests to the bustling streets of Mexico City or the serene coast of Baja California.

Step into our welcoming and vibrant space adorned with colorful decor that reflects the soul of Mexico. Whether you're dining with friends, family, or colleagues, our cozy atmosphere sets the stage for an unforgettable culinary experience. Join us at Little Lemon and embark on a gastronomic journey through Mexico without leaving California. Our friendly staff is dedicated to providing top-notch service, ensuring your dining experience is nothing short of delightful.
